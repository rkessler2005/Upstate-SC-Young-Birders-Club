About the Club

Explore birds, build community, and care for local habitats.

Upstate SC Young Birders Club connects young people through bird walks, photography, conservation projects, and beginner-friendly outdoor learning. Whether you know every warbler by song or just downloaded Merlin, this club is a place to learn, explore, and meet other people who love birds.

Local walks, seasonal sightings, and bird photography across the Upstate.

What We Do

Outings, reports, projects, and resources.

Bird Walks

Monthly beginner-friendly outings at parks, preserves, lakes, and trails around Clemson, Greenville, and the Blue Ridge foothills.

Photo Gallery

Member-submitted bird photography, trip highlights, and seasonal species spotlights from around South Carolina.

Conservation

Bluebird monitoring, habitat cleanups, citizen science projects, and ways to support birds in your own backyard.

Favorite Birding Spots

Start exploring close to home.

Clemson Experimental Forest

Trails, forest edges, lake views, and great opportunities for beginner birding near Clemson.

Conestee Nature Preserve

A major Upstate birding destination with wetlands, boardwalks, and a wide variety of species.

South Cove County Park

A beautiful lakefront spot for watching water birds, songbirds, and seasonal migrants.
