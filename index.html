About the Club

For young birders, quiet mornings, and local wild places.

Upstate SC Young Birders is a welcoming club for young people interested in birding, photography, conservation, and exploring the landscapes of South Carolina. Think state park mornings, field guides, lake trails, and learning birds one walk at a time.

What We Do

Field walks, conservation, and bird photography.

Bird Walks

Beginner friendly walks around Clemson, Greenville, lakes, wetlands, forests, and state parks.

Photography

Member galleries, bird photography tips, and opportunities to document local species.

Conservation

Bluebird boxes, habitat awareness, citizen science, and caring for local bird habitats.

Local Landscape

A soft, quiet Upstate feel.
